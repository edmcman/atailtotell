A Tail to Tell To Close

We are sorry to report that the A Tail to Tell Rescue will close and cease operations in mid 2024.  We helped hundreds of animals find their forever homes, and we are very grateful for all of the donations, time, and support from our volunteers, donors, fosters, and adopters.  We encourage everyone to continue the fight against puppy mills, and to help find a home for all neglected dogs.

The rescue may still be contacted through email at cindy@atailtotell.com.
