Note

Hi! My Name Is Note

We're happy to tell you that Note has a new furever home.
Please visit our Available Dogs Page to see the pups currently available.

Meet Note – Tiny little dude and between 1 and 2 years old

Extremely agile
He will need a good fence. 4 ft or above. If not you could use a run line. He is a love bug and loves to cuddle. He loves all who are in the house.
He will do really well with other dogs. Loves to play with them.
Absolutely no cats!!!!!
He is food reactive so we always fed him separate from our dog but he is okay to give treats to in front of others.
No kids under 13. Only because he gets super excited and can play nip. We have almost eradicated him doing this but I wouldn't trust it. He has tried to nip at our landscaper because he came in the front door. Loves toys and playing fetch. He has been taught place. He will stay in place until you say release however he has not mastered that with big distractions. He is crate trained.
He is house broken.
Loves to walk on leash, but we just usually play with Note in our yard more then take him on walks. He plays hard but will roll over and be submissive to other dogs. He was snippy if you tried to take something from him he shouldn't have, like a tissue or napkin. I have taught him leave it so that isn't really an issue for us anymore but still wouldn't take a chance with little kids. He can and will jump gates in your house if there is something that has triggered his drive. (Our cat).
We adore Note aka Spider. He is loyal to fault. He has proven that he can learn and in fact loves our little training sessions. Oh, he is also protective of me if out and strangers approach. 
He definitely will do best in a home with other pups. . 
To adopt Note, apply at https://www.atailtotell.com/adopt/

Lizzie’s Story

It took some time for her to trust hands and laps, but she is now a loving, happy little lady and a very important part of our family. Believe it or not, she has inspired us to persevere through tough times. It may seem crazy that this fluffy little chihuahua taught us about strength and grit, but we learned that if this little dog could hang in there until she got a chance to live her best life, surely we can make it through a tough day or two.

– Melissa Alfonso
